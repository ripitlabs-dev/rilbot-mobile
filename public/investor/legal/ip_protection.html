Arizona LLC Legal Structure

Entity Formation — Arizona LLC

Rip It Labs, LLC is (or should be) formed under Arizona Revised Statutes § 29-3101 et seq. (Arizona Limited Liability Company Act). Key protections:

  • Personal liability shield for members (Tim Cavin) for company debts and legal claims
  • Pass-through taxation (default) or S-Corp election available
  • Flexible management structure (member-managed or manager-managed)
  • Arizona annual report required — $0 filing fee (cheapest in USA)

Operating Agreement

A comprehensive Operating Agreement should be in place governing:

  • Membership interests (Tim Cavin 100% or as per cap table)
